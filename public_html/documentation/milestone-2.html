Objective

A very short introduction of myself and thanking whoever is reading it. It then explains that this website is my living portfolio to showcase my skills and present my work to employers.

Who Am I

Three sections showing off each facet of who I am: Web Developer, Tinkerer, and Mad Science Teacher. it will have a main picture and then either links to my work, or pictures of my work below each section. there will be three circles here which jump down to that body of work.

Web developer

This will have a list of all the abilities I gained through my bootcamp experience, as well as talking about my history with coding (going all the way back to my game genie!) then it will have links in boxes below to each of the major projects I have completed.

tinkerer

This section will show off all the artsy, nerdy and crafty things I have done. It will include some of my woodworking, model painting, cooking, and costume making. below will be links to learn more about each project.

Mad Science Teacher

This will talk about my 5 years as a science teacher. it will show off some experiments I've run, lesson plans I have made, and maybe add in either the tech stuff I did or the magic club I ran.

Contact Me

A final thank you message followed by a contact form which sends me an email. This will gather their name, phone number, email address, a message, and confirm the message is sent. links to my github and linkedin will also be present on the form and on the footer for the page
