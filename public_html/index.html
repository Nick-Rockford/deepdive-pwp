My pwp

A Portfolio of the projects that define me. This project was a challenge for me. I struggle with Front end Design and this was almost entirely Front end.

My greatest hurdle was in prioritizing what to work on first. I decided to focus on the basic layout and screen reactivity before filling in content. This proved to be the right way to do it because once I finished the layout it was simple to add text and pictures, but it was also stressful because I couldn't see the progress I was making.

If I were to do this again, I would have laid out a timeline on top of my list of objectives. that way, I could better see how much time each part of the project was taking me.

Skills for this project

  • Bootstrap for layout
  • Css for where bootstrap fell short
  • Express for handling contact form integration with mailgun

Frog dissections

My original major was nursing, but when that didn't pan out, I switched to education. no point in letting those higher level biology courses go to waste, so I used what I learned.

I was the first teacher to bring dissections to the school, and it was always the end of year activity to really hammer home everything we had been learning up to that point.

Uses for this project

  • This activity brings the concepts of biology to the real world for students
  • Its a great end of year goal to keep lab safety fresh "If I can't trust you with an X how will I trust you with a scalpel?"
  • For 99% of those students, it will be a fun memory from middle school science
  • For 1% of students it will be the first step in a long pursuit of learning more about the world around them.
  • Two former students credit that lab as the reason they signed up for nursing classes.
Magic Club

A club focused on learning the card game "Magic the gathering"

One area that students struggle in is reading informational texts. This club not only gets my students reading to understand how a card works, but it also means they have to think about the logic of how this card interacts with the logic of the rules.

here are my accomplishments

  • Magic club was the most popular club at the school with over 35 students attending every week
  • We led a successful campaign to raise funds for the club and were awarded $700 to prepare for a tournament
  • Several members of our club have gone on to form a magic club at the high school once they leave our school
